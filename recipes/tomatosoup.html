3 Ingredient Tomato Soup

A bowl of Tomato Soup

Description

You'd never guess that three ingredients is all you need to make a delicious tomato soup, but this recipe will surprise you! Butter, Onion, and Tomatoes go a long way together to make this velvety delight of a tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1/2 large onion, cut into large wedges
  • 1 28-oz. can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cup water, vegetable, or chicken stock
  • Fine sea salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Melt butter over medium heat in a Dutch oven or large saucepan.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add onion wedges, water, can of tomatoes with their juices, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Bring to a simmer. Cook, uncovered, for about 40 minutes. Stir occasionally and add additional salt as needed.

  3. Step 3

    4. Blend the soup, season to taste, and serve!
