Fettucine Alfredo

A plate of fettucine alfredo

Description

Anyone who knows me knows I am a huge pasta nut. Fettucine alfredo was my first foray into cooking as an adult, and it is still one of my go-to recipes when I need something quick, easy, and relatively inexpensive. A garlicky, carb-loaded plate of cheese and pasta hits the spot all throughout the year. Well, except for the two weeks after New Year's Eve, of course

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettucine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic (or 1 dash garlic salt)
  • 3/4 cup grated romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccini and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. Add salt, pepper and garlic. Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted; this will thicken the sauce.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add pasta to sauce. Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
