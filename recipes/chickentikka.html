Chicken Tikka Masala

A plate of Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Indian cuisine is definitely my weakness. Saying no to an Indian dish is just not something that I am capable of. This Chicken Tikka Masala recipe is sure to impress if you're cooking for a crowd, and will offer a meal you'll come back to time and time again, even if you're just cooking for one.

Ingredients

    6 Servings

  • 6 cloves garlic, finely grated
  • 4 tsp. finely grated peeled ginger
  • 4 tsp. ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp. garam masala
  • 2 tsp. ground coriander
  • 2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 cups whole-milk yogurt (not Greek)
  • 1 tbsp. kosher salt
  • 2 lb. skinless, boneless chicken breast, halved lengthwise
  • 3 tbsp. ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion. thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 6 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2 dried chiles de arbol or 1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 28-oz. can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup chopped cilantro, plus sprigs for garnishing
  • steamed basmati rice (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine garlic, ginger, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, and cumin in a small bowl. Whisk yogurt, salt, and half of spice mixture in a medium bowl; add chicken and turn to coat. Cover and chill 4-6 hours. Cover and chill remaining spice mixture.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat ghee in a large heavy pot over medium heat. Add onion, tomato paste, cardamom, and chiles and cook, stirring often, until tomato paste has darkened and onion is soft, about 5 minutes. Add remaining half of spice mixture and cook, stirring often, until bottom of pot begins to brown, about 4 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add tomatoes with juices, crushing them with your hands as you add them. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, stirring often and scraping up browned bits from bottom of pot, until sauce thickens, 8-10 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Add cream and chopped cilantro. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens, 30-40 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Meanwhile, preheat broiler. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil and set a wire rack inside sheet. Arrange chicken on rack in a single layer. Broil until chicken starts to blacken in spots (it will not be cooked through), about 10 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces, add to sauce, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until chicken is cooked through, 8-10 minutes. Serve with rice and cilantro sprigs
