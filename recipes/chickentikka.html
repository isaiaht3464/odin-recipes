Chicken Tikka Masala

A plate of Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Indian cuisine is definitely my weakness. Saying no to an Indian dish is just not something that I am capable of. This Chicken Tikka Masala recipe is sure to impress if you're cooking for a crowd, and will offer a meal you'll come back to time and time again, even if you're just cooking for yourself

Ingredients

    6 Servings

  • 6 cloves garlic, finely grated
  • 4 tsp. finely grated peeled ginger
  • 4 tsp. ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp. garam masala
  • 2 tsp. ground coriander
  • 2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 cups whole-milk yogurt (not Greek)

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccini and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. Add salt, pepper and garlic. Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted; this will thicken the sauce.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add pasta to sauce. Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
